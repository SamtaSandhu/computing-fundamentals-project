Fundamentals of Computing

CMPS1134 - Final Project

This course is an introduction to the fundamentals of Computer Science that will provide students with a fundation for future courses in Computer Science. This course is a board survey of the discipline, covering essential topics such as computer architecture, operating systems, and computer networks; software and software development; data organization.

Team Mission

Our team is dedicated to demonstrating the practical application of data structures through interactive visualization. This website serves as our final project portfolio, showcasing our understanding of Stack and Queue data structures and their real world implementations.
